UTA Records is a conglomeration of ideas, which defines Art Music - a cultivation of sounds and tradition.

As the dominant audio recording player throughout most of the 20th century, the phonograph record player is still in continued use with niche audiences today. Its prevalence and notoriety in modern culture is representative of UTA Records and their mission to be a revolutionary force in the university music recording industry.
